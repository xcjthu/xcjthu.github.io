About me

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University. I am advised by Professor Zhiyuan Liu and affiliated with the Natural Language Processing Lab (THUNLP). My research interests lie within the intersection of natural language processing, deep learning, information extraction and Legal AI. Before I becoming a Ph.D. student, I also received my bachelor degree from Tsinghua University.
